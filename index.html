Global Canvas Art Exhibition Concert 2023

Our goal is to bring the beauty and diversity of art to the world by showcasing the work of talented artists from around the globe.

2023.04.03(MON) ~ 6(THUR)

@ Nairobi National Museum Nairobi, Kenya.

Main Program

Meet the Artists

An opening night event where visitors can meet the artists, learn about their work and processes, and engage in Q&A sessions.

Workshop Series

A series of interactive workshops led by the artists, covering a variety of topics such as painting techniques, printmaking, and sculpture.

Artist Talks

An opportunity for artists to give presentations about their work and share their experiences and insights with visitors.

Art Tour

A tour of the galleries, allowing visitors to get a closer look at the artwork and learn more about the artists and their work.

Closing Celebration

A finale event with a closing reception and the announcement of awards for the most exceptional artwork.
